# 📋 VAMPEES: Integrated Nutrient Network

This manifest documents the standardized biological assets required for long-term crew sustainability in orbital stations and surface domes. 

---

## 1. Primary Caloric & Vitamin Anchors
*   **Sweet Potatoes (*Ipomoea batatas*):** Primary source of dietary beta-carotene (Vitamin A) and complex carbohydrates. Edible foliage serves as an iron-rich secondary green.
*   **Solanum Potatoes:** High-density caloric staple for bulk energy. High potassium and baseline Vitamin C.
*   **Soybeans (*Glycine max*):** The structural protein anchor. Provides complete protein profiles and essential nitrogen-fixing capabilities for soil revitalization.

## 2. Macro-Algae & Aquatic Infrastructure
*   **Emerald Trench Arrays (Kelp variants):** Multi-story aquatic corridors providing essential Iodine and trace mineral payloads.
*   **Dulse & Nori (Rhodophyta):** Rapid-growth sea vegetables used for umami-flavor profile enhancement, protein density, and B12 synthesis.

## 3. 🍄 Decomposer & Nutrient Recycling
*   **Fungal Arrays:** Primary decomposers for agricultural fiber and biomass. Provides critical umami flavor compounds and high-density protein without light-load requirements.

## 4. 🦐 Symbiotic Maintenance Matrix (The "Cleanup Crew")
*   **Neocaridina Shrimp:** Detritivores that maintain water clarity and bio-film regulation within aquatic systems.
*   **Gastropod Arrays (Snails):** Essential for plexiglass transparency maintenance and calcium-rich waste processing for soil mineralization.

## 5. 🌹 Pharmaceutical & Psychological Assets
*   **Rose Arrays (*Rosa*):** High-efficiency synthesis of Vitamin C via rose hip cultivation. Serves as a vital biophilic element for crew psychological health under confinement.
*   **Shrub-Tier Beverages (*Camellia sinensis* / *Coffea*):** Vertically-integrated beverage crops for caffeine and psychological morale, managed as compact shrub arrays to minimize spatial overhead.

## 6. 🥬 Micronutrient & Green Supplements
*   **Mung Beans:** High-speed sprout production for essential B-vitamin supplementation and rapid dietary fresh-greens.
*   **Standardized Micro-Algae:** Continuous oxygenation and primary water-filtration engine for life-support loops.

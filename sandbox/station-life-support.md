# Station Life Support: Decoupled Algae Matrices

## 1. Absolute Spatial and Atmospheric Isolation
The station relies on a minimum of 6 to 10 entirely independent Algae Matrix rooms. There is no physical connection, shared piping, or direct air cross-over between these rooms. 

* **The Air Firewall:** Carbon dioxide entering from the station and oxygen being pumped back out must pass through dedicated, independent molecular sieves and multi-stage submicron particulate filters unique to that specific room. 
* **The Zero-Leak Mandate:** If a biological crash or contamination occurs in Matrix 3, it is completely impossible for the pathogen to drift into Matrix 4. The rooms are physically separated by heavy structural bulkheads.

---

## 2. Human Vector Containment (The Clean Room Protocol)
Humans are walking biohazards covered in skin flakes, bacteria, and fungal spores. To protect the cultivars, no human may simply walk into an Algae Matrix room.

* **Multi-Stage Airlocks:** Access requires passing through a strict three-stage clean room protocol:
    1. **Stage 1 (Decontamination):** Removal of standard station clothing, full antimicrobial wash, and donning of a sterile, full-body bunny suit with an independent, closed-loop respirator.
    2. **Stage 2 (The Air Shower):** High-velocity, HEPA-filtered air knives blast the technician from all angles to strip away any micro-particulates or loose fibers.
    3. **Stage 3 (Sterile Entry):** Step-in sticky mats to grab boot contaminants before the final pressure-sealed door opens into the Matrix.

---

## 3. Feed and Nutrient Security (The Inbound Scanner)
Algae cannot live on carbon dioxide alone; it requires a constant influx of water, nitrogen, phosphorus, and trace micronutrients. This material represents the highest risk for introducing a systemic contaminant.

* **The Inline Bio-Scanner:** Every drop of water and every batch of nutrient media meant to feed the Matrix must pass through an automated diagnostic checkpoint before entering the clean lines. 
* **The Check:** High-throughput automated mass spectrometry and rapid DNA-sequencing loops scan the fluid in real-time. The system cross-references the input against a database of known predatory phages, competitive molds, and chemical toxins.
* **The Intercept:** If even a single foreign biological marker or anomalous chemical signature is detected, the intake valve slam-shuts in milliseconds. The contaminated feed batch is instantly diverted to an isolated containment tank, blasted with superheated steam, and purged entirely out of the system.

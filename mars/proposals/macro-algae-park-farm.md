# 🍀 The Integrated Nutrient Network: Martian/Orbital Infrastructure

## 1. Systemic Philosophy
The Nutrient Network operates as a closed-loop biological architecture. Food production, life-support maintenance, and human psychological health are treated as a singular, unified cycle rather than independent systems.

## 2. Infrastructure: The Emerald Trench System
To resolve the competing needs of radiation shielding, dietary mineral requirements, and psychological stability, the colony utilizes the "Emerald Trench" architecture.
* **Architectural Integration:** Public walking paths are constructed beneath or adjacent to multi-story plexiglass enclosures filled with high-density macro-kelp forests. 
* **Radiation Shielding:** These massive water volumes act as a hydrogen-rich firewall, absorbing cosmic radiation and allowing citizens to utilize public spaces safely under natural light.
* **Macro-Algae Harvesting:** The forest is not merely decorative; it is a high-output farm where kelp is harvested for human dietary use, specifically to provide essential iodine and mineral payloads for thyroid and metabolic health.
* **Civic Biophilia:** The immersive environment—characterized by swaying kelp, filtered light, and living aquatic ecosystems—provides critical psychological relief from long-term confinement.

## 3. Symbiotic Maintenance Matrix
* **Bioremediation Engine:** The trench ecosystem is managed by an integrated population of *Neocaridina* (shrimp) and gastropods (snails), which act as self-regulating cleaners, consuming biofilms and detritus from the glass and plant surfaces.
* **Soil & Fertilization Loop:** Beyond serving as human rations, excess kelp biomass and processed invertebrate waste are routed into terrestrial agricultural zones to provide nutrient-rich, organic fertilization for staple crops like potatoes and beans.

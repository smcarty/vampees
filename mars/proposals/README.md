# 📑 Mars Node: Technical Proposals

Welcome to the active incubation and review floor for the Martian sector. This directory is where specific, focused engineering designs, closed-loop life support architectures, soil remediation techniques, and early-stage moon logistics variants live while they are being actively audited, stress-tested, and honed by the community.

If an idea has graduated from the raw, open-ended universal sandbox and has a dedicated blueprint layout specifically for the Martian surface, Phobos, or Deimos, it belongs here.

---

## 🔬 The Peer Review & Graduation Process

Files in this folder are treated as serious engineering candidates. To help a design graduate out of this temporary proposals folder and into the core Martian framework files, the community will collaborate directly in the pull requests and discussion threads to verify three things:

1. **The Environmental Check:** Do the atmospheric extraction numbers, radiation-shielding depths, or automated habitat pressure seals hold up against the harsh, perchlorate-heavy Martian environment?
2. **The Self-Sufficiency Index:** Does the system achieve maximum mechanical or biological recycling efficiency, proving it can operate sustainably without relying on a permanent cargo lifeline from Earth?
3. **The Open-Source Safeguard:** Are the structural designs and orbital tracking paths built on open data protocols, ensuring the Martian frontier remains a shared public trust completely immune to corporate claims or private colonization monopolies.

# AUTHORS_NOTE.md

> "We must learn to be caretakers before we are allowed to be explorers."

If you can't tell, this framework wasn't built by a corporate think-tank, a military contractor, or a billionaire's board of directors. It was put together by a craftswoman and an artist who dreads watching humanity repeat its worst mistakes. 

I like to dream. I want to see another planet in the future, and I desperately want to dream that there is a world where humans can thrive together while preserving nature and the universe.

> **Colonization must never happen.**

Our planet is teetering on the edge of a great chasm dug out through greed, corporate extraction, and the systemic destruction of cultures, peoples, and ecosystems. The blueprints in this repository exist to prove a point: we can design the hard technology to reach the stars *without* bringing our history of violence and ecocide with us. 

If humanity leaves Earth under a "Manifest Destiny" or corporate-wealth mindset, we are just packing up the old European colonial playbook and taking it to other planets to strip-mine the universe. This architecture is an explicit ideological protest against that future. It is a blueprint for exploration, civilizational humility, and public cooperation instead of conquest.

If you are here to exploit, privatize, or claim-stake the cosmos for the enrichment of the few at the expense of the universe, turn around. This project belongs to the future of a kinder, wiser humanity.

*— smcarty*

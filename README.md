## Executive Summary

The colonization and terraforming of Mars have traditionally been bottlenecked by a fundamental thermodynamic paradox: Mars is a geologically dead, resource-starved planet. The proposal that we extract materials from Earth are structurally unviable; Earth has no surplus mass to spare without triggering ecological collapse.

The **VAMPEES** architecture treats the inner solar system as a unified chemical refinery and energy grid. By leveraging the environmental excesses of Venus (excess solar radiation and atmospheric mass) and the raw materials of the Main Asteroid Belt (water ice and mineral aggregate), we can systematically terraform Mars and build a permanent multi-planetary economic loop without robbing Earth—and without relying on private corporate billionaires to get us there.

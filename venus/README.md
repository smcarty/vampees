# ☁️ Venus Node: Atmosphere, Energy, and Infrastructure

Welcome to the primary directory for the Venusian sector of the VAMPEES architecture. This folder serves as the active workshop for mapping out the floating aerostat cities, atmospheric energy harvesting, and magnetic mass driver systems required to power the interplanetary pipeline.

---

## 🔬 Active Workshops & Proposals

To maintain a clean framework, files in this directory are split by their developmental status:

### 📑 1. Core Framework (The Main Files)
These are our working blueprints. They hold the core concepts we are actively honing, cross-pollinating, and stress-testing against hard physics. 

### 📂 2. The `proposals/` Subfolder (The Specialized Sandbox)
When a concept graduates out of the main, universal `sandbox/` directory and proves it has a dedicated, viable application specifically for Venus, it is moved into our local `proposals/` folder. 
* **What lives here:** High-potential engineering concepts, atmospheric buoyancy math, or solar harvesting variants that are undergoing deep community audit.
* **The Goal:** We stress-test these proposals here until the physics are seamless, the resource metrics balance out, and they are ready to be integrated directly into the core Venusian framework.

---

## 🪐 Local Core Objectives

Every file and proposal cultivated in this directory must align with the primary mission of the Venus Node:
1. **The Energy Engine:** Developing sustainable, high-yield energy collection systems within the sulfuric acid layers and upper atmosphere.
2. **The Logistics Link:** Designing the atmospheric-to-orbit export mechanisms (like mass drivers or orbital tethers) to feed energy packets into the broader system.
3. **Decentralized Public Trust:** Ensuring all physical assets and structural data paths remain entirely open-source and structurally immune to corporate or monopolistic capture.
